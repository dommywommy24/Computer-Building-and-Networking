Axolotls are different to normal fish
Axolotls have some differences when it comes to caring for one yourself.

Tank

  • An Axolotl's tank floor space is more important than height
  • Water temperature should be between 60°F to 65°F
  • pH should be between 6.5 and 8

Diet

  • Bloodworms
  • Frozen Brine
  • Live Nightcrawlers
  • Mysis Shrimp
  • Red Wigglers

Behavior

  • Mostly active at night
  • Slow-moving and Docile
  • Will hang out in the open when relaxed
  • Tend to be anti-social with other Axolotls
